Personal Perspective

Why I Want to Go to ENC

I want to go to École nationale de cirque because it represents the level of training, discipline, and artistic excellence that I want to reach as a circus artist.

World-class training

ENC stands out to me because it is known for serious professional training. I do not just want a place where I can practice circus. I want a school that pushes artists technically, physically, and creatively at a very high level.

Cirque du Soleil goal

One of my biggest goals is to give myself the strongest possible chance of reaching the highest level of performance, including opportunities connected to Cirque du Soleil and other world-class productions. ENC feels like one of the clearest paths toward that kind of future.

My disciplines

The disciplines that matter most to me are partner acro and straps. Both require strength, trust, control, timing, and discipline. I want to keep developing those skills in an environment that takes them seriously and helps turn potential into professional-level performance.

What I want to bring back

I also care about what happens after the training. I want to bring back higher-level knowledge, stronger skills, and bigger ideas to the circus community in Boston and Massachusetts. I want the experience to help me grow, but I also want it to help me contribute more to the community around me.

Why this matters to me personally

ENC is not just a school I think is cool. It feels like the kind of place that could genuinely change the level of my training, expand my future in circus, and move me closer to the kind of performer and artist I want to become.
