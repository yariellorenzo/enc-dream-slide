Why ENC

Why this school stands out to me

I want to go to ENC because it feels like the school that gives me the strongest chance to reach the level I am aiming for. I do not just want to be good at circus — I want to become world-class.

Why I want to go here

I want to go to ENC because it is my dream school. If I want to get closer to the kind of performance level I connect with Cirque du Soleil, I believe this school gives me one of the best chances to do that. I want to build the kind of skills that people remember, and I want to earn stories from real training, real struggle, and real performance.

Why it matters beyond just me

I also think the people and connections around ENC could matter in a bigger way. Growing around serious artists and coaches in that environment could help me build relationships that matter later — especially if I come back and want to help the circus community in Boston and Massachusetts grow stronger too.

What separates ENC from other schools

  • It feels more serious, artistic, and elite.
  • It is in Montreal, a city with a deep circus culture.
  • It is known for shaping complete performers, not just teaching tricks.
  • It feels closer to the level of excellence I want to reach.
