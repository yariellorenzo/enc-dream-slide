Why ENC

Why this school carries so much weight

ENC stands out because it is not just a place to learn tricks. It is a serious circus school in a city with a strong circus identity, and it is associated with a high-level artistic and professional culture.

Serious artistic and technical training

One of the biggest reasons ENC stands out is that it is known for combining physical discipline with artistic development. The training is not only about being strong or skilled. It is also about performance quality, creativity, control, and becoming a complete circus artist.

Montreal’s circus ecosystem

The school also benefits from being in Montreal, which has a much stronger circus identity than most cities. That gives ENC a bigger cultural presence and connects it to a wider world of performers, companies, coaches, and artistic inspiration.

What makes ENC feel different

  • It is recognized as a serious professional circus school.
  • It is rooted in a city with major circus influence.
  • It develops both technical skill and artistic identity.
  • It is often associated with a higher level of performance ambition.
