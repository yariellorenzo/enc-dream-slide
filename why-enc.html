Why ENC

Why the school stands out

ENC stands out because it is not only a place for skill development. It is a serious circus school in a city with a strong circus identity, and it is associated with a high-level artistic and professional environment.

Serious artistic and technical training

One of the clearest reasons ENC stands out is its reputation for combining physical discipline with artistic development. Training is not limited to strength or technique alone. Performance quality, creativity, control, and artistic identity are also central parts of the environment.

Montreal’s circus ecosystem

The school also benefits from being in Montreal, which has a much stronger circus identity than most cities. That gives ENC a larger cultural presence and places it closer to a wider world of performers, companies, coaches, and artistic inspiration.

Distinctive characteristics

  • Recognized as a serious professional circus school.
  • Located in a city with major circus influence.
  • Develops both technical skill and artistic identity.
  • Associated with a high level of performance ambition.
