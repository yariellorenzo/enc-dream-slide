Training Disciplines

Partner acro and straps

These disciplines reflect the type of physical and artistic ability that a school like ENC is built to develop.

Partner Acro

Partner acro combines strength, timing, trust, balance, and difficult movement. It shows how circus performance can be technical while still being exciting to watch. A discipline like this matches a school environment that values both physical ability and performance quality.

Straps

Straps is built on power, control, endurance, and clean movement. It is a strong example of the kind of discipline where technical mastery and visual impact matter at the same time. That balance fits well with the kind of professional circus training ENC is known for.
