My Disciplines

Partner acro and straps

These are the disciplines that feel most connected to the kind of performer I want to become.

Partner Acro

What I like about partner acro is that it is both physically challenging and exciting to watch. There is a mix of flips, balances, standing on other people, timing, trust, and difficult tricks that make it feel intense and impressive at the same time. It takes real control, and that is part of why I respect it so much.

Straps

Straps is pure strength, movement, and control. The twisting, turning, holding, and power in it makes it feel like one of the clearest examples of making the impossible look possible. That combination of force and beauty is a huge reason why it matters to me.
