Why ENC

Why I chose this school

I want to go to ENC because it feels like the school that gives me the strongest shot at becoming the kind of performer I want to be. My dream is not just to train hard — it is to reach a level where I can perform with companies like Cirque du Soleil, travel, go on tour, and build real stories out of my work. I also care about what happens after that. The people, coaches, and connections around ENC could help me grow not only as an artist, but also as someone who can bring more back to the circus community in Boston and Massachusetts later on.

What makes it feel right for me

  • It is internationally respected and connected to a serious circus culture in Montreal.
  • It feels closer to the highest level of artistic and physical training.
  • I want a place that pushes me hard and treats circus as both performance and discipline.

What I want from the experience

  • To become stronger, cleaner, and more complete as a performer.
  • To build unforgettable stories from training, touring, and performing.
  • To grow with other people who are as serious about circus as I am.
ENC Facts

What the school is, where it is, and what it is known for

École nationale de cirque, also called the National Circus School, is a professional circus arts school in Montreal, Quebec. It has been training circus artists since 1981 and is known for high-level training in an environment built specifically for circus. It is part of Montreal’s larger circus ecosystem, which is one reason the city matters so much to me.

LocationMontreal, Quebec, Canada

Montreal is one of the strongest circus cities in North America, with a deep creative and performance culture.

ProgramsPreparatory, secondary, and higher-education circus training

The school offers a full ladder of circus education, including professional college-level training.

College experienceIts college-level circus diploma is a three-year program

This is the part that matters most to me because it combines high-level circus specialization with a serious training path.

Training styleArtistic + technical + physical

ENC is known for shaping complete performers, not just teaching isolated skills.

Community & campusPart of Montreal’s Cité des arts du cirque

It sits inside a circus hub that includes major circus institutions and a strong surrounding artistic community.

My Disciplines

Why partner acro and straps matter to me

Partner Acro

I like partner acro because it is physically challenging and powerful to watch. There is trust, timing, balance, flips, standing on other people, and difficult skills that look almost unreal when they are done well. It takes both control and courage, and that is part of why I love it.

Straps

I like straps because it is pure strength, control, and movement. Twisting, turning, holding shapes, and making hard things look smooth is part of what draws me to it. Straps feels like proof that what looks impossible can actually become possible through training.
