École nationale de cirque

ENC

École nationale de cirque is a professional circus school in Montreal, Quebec, known for serious artistic and physical training. It stands out because it exists inside one of the strongest circus environments in North America and is connected to a culture of high-level performance.

Explore the School Why It Stands Out

What this site covers

This site focuses on what ENC is, where it is, what kind of training it offers, what makes it respected, and how disciplines like partner acro and straps connect to the type of circus artist the school helps develop.

Location

Montreal, Quebec

ENC is based in one of the best-known circus cities in North America, which gives it a strong cultural and professional context.

Reputation

Professional circus training

The school is known for serious preparation in technique, artistry, and performance, not just isolated skill practice.

Focus

Complete performer development

What makes ENC compelling is how it develops artists who need strength, discipline, control, creativity, and stage presence all at once.
