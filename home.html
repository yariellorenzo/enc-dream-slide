École nationale de cirque

ENC

École nationale de cirque is a professional circus school in Montreal, Quebec, recognized for serious artistic and physical training. The school is notable for existing inside one of the strongest circus environments in North America and for being associated with high-level performance culture.

About the School Why ENC

Site overview

This site presents core information about ENC, including its location, training structure, professional reputation, artistic environment, and representative disciplines within circus education.

Location

Montreal, Quebec

ENC is located in a city widely recognized for its deep connection to circus arts and performance culture.

Reputation

Professional circus training

The school is known for serious preparation in technique, artistry, and performance rather than isolated skill practice alone.

Focus

Complete performer development

ENC emphasizes strength, discipline, control, creativity, and stage presence as part of complete performer development.
