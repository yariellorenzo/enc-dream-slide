About the School

What École nationale de cirque is

ENC is a professional circus arts school in Montreal, Quebec. It is known for serious circus training and for helping develop strong performers in a city with a major circus identity.

Location

Montreal, Quebec, Canada

Montreal is one of the biggest circus centers in North America and has a strong creative performance culture.

Founded

1981

The school has decades of history training circus artists.

Programs

Preparatory, secondary, and higher education

ENC offers multiple levels of circus training, including college-level professional preparation.

College training

Three-year professional diploma path

This is one of the biggest reasons I am drawn to it — it is a serious multi-year path, not something casual.

Training style

Physical, technical, and artistic

ENC is known for building complete artists, not just strong athletes.

Campus / community

Part of Montreal’s circus ecosystem

The school is tied to a bigger circus environment, which adds to why it feels so important to me.
